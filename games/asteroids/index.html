How to Play

  • Destroy all asteroids to advance to the next wave.
  • Large asteroids split into smaller ones when hit.
  • You have 3 lives. Colliding with an asteroid costs a life.
  • Your ship wraps around the screen edges.

Controls

  • ← → — Rotate ship
  • ↑ — Thrust
  • Space — Shoot
  • Touch buttons — Mobile controls
