How to Play

  • Click a line between dots to draw it.
  • Or drag from dot to adjacent dot!
  • Completing the 4th side of a box claims it for you.
  • If you complete a box, you get an extra turn.
  • The player with more boxes at the end wins!
  • Choose vs CPU or 2 Player mode.
