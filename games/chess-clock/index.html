How to Use

  • Choose a time preset or set custom minutes/increment.
  • Click Start to begin. Player 1's clock starts running.
  • Tap/click your clock after making a move to pass the turn.
  • Increment time (if set) is added after each move.
  • Use ⏸ to pause and ↻ to reset.
