How to Play

  • Ships are placed randomly on both grids.
  • Click a cell on the Enemy Waters grid to fire.
  • 🔥 = Hit, 💨 = Miss.
  • Sink all 5 enemy ships (sizes 5, 4, 3, 3, 2) to win.
  • The enemy fires back after each of your shots.
  • First to sink all ships wins!
