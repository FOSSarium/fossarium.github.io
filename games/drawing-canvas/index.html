Drawing Canvas

How to Use

  • Draw by clicking/touching and dragging on the canvas.
  • Choose a color from the palette below.
  • Adjust brush size with the slider.
  • Use ✏️ Pen to draw or 🧹 Eraser to erase.
  • Undo reverts your last stroke.
  • Clear removes everything from canvas.
  • Export saves your artwork as a PNG file.
