How to Play

  • Move the paddle to bounce the ball and break all bricks.
  • You have 3 lives. Losing the ball costs a life.
  • Clear all bricks to advance to the next level.
  • Higher levels = faster ball + more bricks!

Controls

  • Mouse / Touch — Move paddle
  • ← → keys — Move paddle
  • Click or tap to launch the ball.
