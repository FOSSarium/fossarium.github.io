How to Play

  • Two players (🔴 Red and 🔵 Blue) take turns.
  • Move your marbles from your triangle to the opposite triangle.
  • Click a marble, then click a highlighted space to move.
  • Move one space, or hop over adjacent marbles (chain hops allowed!).
  • First player to fill the opposite triangle wins.
