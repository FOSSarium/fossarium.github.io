How to Play

  • Place stones on intersections to claim territory.
  • Surround opponent stones to capture them.
  • Control more territory than your opponent to win.
  • Stones with no liberties (empty adjacent points) are captured.
  • Pass when you have no good moves left.
  • Game ends when both players pass consecutively.
