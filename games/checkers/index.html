How to Play

  • You play as Red; the CPU plays as Blue.
  • Click a piece, then click a highlighted square to move.
  • Pieces move diagonally. Jumps are mandatory.
  • Reach the far row to become a King (can move both directions).
  • Capture all enemy pieces or block them to win!
