How It Works

  • Left-click on the canvas to place food sources (green).
  • Right-click to place walls/obstacles (draw by dragging).
  • Ants leave the nest (brown circle) and search for food.
  • When an ant finds food, it carries it back and leaves a pheromone trail.
  • Other ants follow strong pheromone trails, creating efficient paths.
  • Adjust ant count and speed with the sliders.
